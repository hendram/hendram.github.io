Mobile Ads Car Analytics

Route cost intelligence and real-time analytics platform for mobile advertising vehicles.

Inspiration

Years ago during a traffic jam, I saw a mobile advertising car stuck on the road. That moment sparked a question: what happens to advertising cost efficiency when the vehicle never reaches its destination on time?

If delays increase operational cost, businesses need a way to predict, analyze, and optimize route expenses before deployment. This project was built to solve that problem for mobile advertising fleets.

What It Does

  • Analyzes cost across routes
  • Calculates cost per meter
  • Compares cost between vehicles
  • Evaluates cost per driver
  • Breaks down cost per waypoint
  • Detects anomalies in trip spending

How It Was Built

  • Real-time analysis agent powered by Google ADK
  • Vehicle monitoring system capturing video and route telemetry
  • Trip data stored in Firestore
  • Analytics engine processes historical + live movement data

Key Analytical Capabilities

Driver Cost Comparison

The system can compare route cost between multiple vehicles with different pricing models.

  • Car 1 → $0.22 per 10 km
  • Car 2 → $0.30 per 20 km

Returns:

  • Total cost per route
  • Waypoint breakdown tables
  • Cost difference analysis
  • Most expensive vehicle identification

Trip Intelligence

  • Total trips per vehicle
  • Driver time-on-road comparison
  • Latest trip analytics

Fuel Cost Analysis

Supports multiple pricing models:
  • Cost per km
  • Cost per meter
  • Variable rate per vehicle
Outputs:
  • Leg-level cost tables
  • Total fuel cost
  • Graph coordinate output for visualization
  • Anomaly detection datasets
